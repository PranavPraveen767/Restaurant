LOCATION

Nestled within the vibrant pulse and hustle and bustle of the energetic and lively area of Kakkanad, the luxurious and elegant Lake Palace hotel and restaurant, with its ornate and opulent interiors and atmosphere of refinement, invites and welcomes you to enter into a domain and sphere where mouthwatering and appetizing culinary delights creatively prepared by skilled chefs using fresh local ingredients meld and intermix seamlessly with attentive, prompt and courteous service delivered by a knowledgeable and professional wait staff. Our establishment, with its beautiful architecture and design along with our commitment to excellence, quality and attention to detail, stands as a paragon and prime example of the art, science and craft of meticulously creating and preparing flavorful gastronomic creations and dishes that stimulate and excite the taste buds and olfactory senses while also satisfying the appetite and warming the heart and spirit.
